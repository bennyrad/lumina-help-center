How do I complete my Profile and Preference Center?

View in Help Scout

Profile setup is managed in two locations on the platform: Manage Profile and Preference Center.

Manage Profile (Owner or Admin)

Open Profile → Manage Profile in the left sidebar.

  • Profile Details. Basic Profile information, including intro statement and bio.
  • Personalize. Targeted questions covering interests, images, Lookbook items, noteworthy articles, and social media links.
  • Calendar Events. Upcoming events and cultural moments the Profile is involved in or attending.

Preference Center (Owner)

Open Profile → Preference Center in the left sidebar.

The Preference Center captures preference and taste-based responses that inform matching between Talent and Partner Profiles.

Editing

Profile details and preferences can be edited at any time.
