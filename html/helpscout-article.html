Who can see my Profile during Beta?

During Beta, only a select group of Talent and Partner Profiles will be on the platform, as Lumina is invite-only and we're carefully curating who joins.

Talent Profiles can only view, search, and connect with Partner Profiles, and vice versa. Talent Profiles are only visible on Lumina if they are claimed by the Talent and published, while Partner Profiles don't need to be claimed to be visible.

Who can see your Profile depends on your visibility settings (more on that in our Privacy section below). However, you can't interact with other Profiles until you "Express interest" and the other Profile accepts your request.
